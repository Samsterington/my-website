Hi there!

I'm Sam and I'm The Coding Coach, thank you for visiting my website. I consider myself both a tutor and a mentor, helping others to develop their skills and understanding of logic, programming, and maths, as well as combining these into a creative outlook.

I coach people of all ages and skills, from children with a passion for all things technology to those who struggle with maths and need to find a different, more practical and artistic way of approaching it. I also coach adults who want to explore the exciting world of programming but aren’t sure where to begin.

I tailor my coaching to the needs of each specific student but with a unified aim of making sessions entertaining, creative and engaging. Some say coding is boring, but with my methods, focusing on visual and imaginative coding, you'll be amazed how easily you’ll get hooked. Want to see some examples? This background is something that you will be able to create after just one session, or have a look at the Your Work! section to see what some of my amazing students have created.
