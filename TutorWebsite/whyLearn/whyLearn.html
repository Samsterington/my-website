Why learn to code?

There are many benifits of learning how to code.


  • Develop your mind to think logically.
  • Grow your ability at problem solving, for studying, exams and work.
  • Learn and practice maths and physics in a practical setting.
  • Gain an understanding of how the technology that runs the modern world actually works.
  • Enjoy the fun in designing and building your own websites and programs.
  • Joining the fastest growing major industry in the world: computing and technology!

The main reasons for learning to code will vary hugely between each student; thus, my sessions and ways of teaching differ for each students needs. The pacing, type of activities and format of our sessions adapt depending on what you want to get out of them.
