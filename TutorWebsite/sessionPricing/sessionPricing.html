Sessions and Pricing

I normally coach between one and three students at a time and sessions generally last an hour, though they can be extended on request. I happily travel to my students homes for free if they are within reasonable cycling distance, and if not I’m also happy to drive; I just ask my petrol costs are covered. All you will need is WiFi and a computer/laptop to code on, and if you don’t have a personal computer I can bring a spare laptop along. If you don’t live in Cambridge I can also coach you online. I do this using a video chat and a shared online coding space which allows both myself and the student to edit the same code in realtime.

Session hourly prices:
Private coaching : £30
Small group coaching (2/3) : £36/£42
Large group coaching : Situation dependent

Contact me either by email or phone if you need any other information about the sessions or would like to arrange one. I hope to hear from you soon.
